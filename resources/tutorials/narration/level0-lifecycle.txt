Every person in the database has a lifecycle status. There are four. <break time="0.6s" /> Pending engagement means the person is new. They have just registered, but they are not yet actively involved. They are waiting to be welcomed and brought on board. <break time="0.6s" /> Active means the person is currently involved — taking part, paying, or serving. <break time="0.5s" /> Dormant means the person has gone quiet. They are still in the system, still a member or a volunteer, but they have not been active for a while. They have not left — they may simply need to be re-engaged. <break time="0.7s" /> Archived means the person has left the system. They are no longer counted as a member or a volunteer, and they are set aside from the active records. <break time="0.6s" /> So, pending, active, and dormant are all "still with us" — the difference is only where the person is on their journey.
