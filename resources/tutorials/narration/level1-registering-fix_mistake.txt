Mistakes happen.
<break time="0.6s" />
A record can't be edited — so you fix it in two steps.
<break time="0.6s" />
First, withdraw the record.
<break time="0.6s" />
Then, add a new one with the correct details.
<break time="0.6s" />
If you don't see a Withdraw button, the record has already been approved.
<break time="0.6s" />
In that case, contact your branch — they can delete it for you.
