Branches can also have organisations as members — companies or institutions.
<break time="0.6s" />
They can make donations, pay membership fees, get certificates, and receive campaign messages.
<break time="0.6s" />
Unlike persons, an organisation can't register itself. It can only be added by a branch.
<break time="0.6s" />
Open Organisations, press "Add Organisation", and fill in the form.
<break time="0.6s" />
It's necessary to link persons to the organisation.
<break time="0.6s" />
If you link more than one, mark one of them as the primary contact.
<break time="0.6s" />
From the organisation's page, you can add payments and donations, and view its certificates and campaigns.
