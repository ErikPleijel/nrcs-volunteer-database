Depending on your access level, you can use this system for:
<break time="0.6s" />
Payments — Register and track individual membership payments.
<break time="0.6s" />
Volunteering — Record volunteering hours.
<break time="0.6s" />
Donations — Register individual and organisational donations to the Society.
<break time="0.6s" />
Trainings — Track first-aid certifications and other trainings.
<break time="0.6s" />
Campaigns — Draft and send bulk email or SMS messages.
<break time="0.6s" />
ID-cards and Certificates — Prepare and print membership ID-cards and training certificates.
