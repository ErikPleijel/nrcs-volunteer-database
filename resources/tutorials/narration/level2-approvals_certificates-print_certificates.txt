There are two ways to print a certificate.
<break time="0.6s" />
One at a time — go to Persons, find the person, click View, then scroll to the relevant record, and click Print certificate.
<break time="0.6s" />
This brings you to the Certificate Generator page.
<break time="0.6s" />
You can also print in bulk, right from that same page.
<break time="0.6s" />
Tick the certificates you want, or click Select All.
<break time="0.6s" />
Then choose "Print for pre-printed paper", or "Print with logo and frame".
<break time="0.6s" />
Pre-printed paper assumes you already have branded stationery — it only prints the text.
<break time="0.6s" />
Print with logo and frame prints the full certificate design.
