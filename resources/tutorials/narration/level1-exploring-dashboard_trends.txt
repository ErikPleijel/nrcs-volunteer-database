Further down the dashboard, you'll find Trends and Statistics.
<break time="0.6s" />
These are detailed reports on membership, volunteers, finances, donations, training, and registrations.
<break time="0.6s" />
Below that, the Heat Maps show bubble charts.
<break time="0.6s" />
The bigger the bubble, the more activity in that branch or division.
<break time="0.6s" />
It's a quick way to see where things are thriving, and where they might need attention.
