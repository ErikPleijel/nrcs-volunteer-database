There's an easy way to start a campaign — the Campaign filter wizard.
<break time="0.6s" />
On Persons, click "Campaign filter wizard", then pick a goal.
<break time="0.6s" />
For example, welcoming newly registered persons, re-engaging dormant volunteers, or reminding people about expiring membership.
<break time="0.6s" />
Answer a few simple questions — like who to include, or how recently they were last contacted.
<break time="0.6s" />
Then click "Apply filter and close". The right filters are set for you automatically.
