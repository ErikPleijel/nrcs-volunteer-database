Training works the same way. <break time="0.5s" /> Open Training Records, press Add, and select the person — just as you did for volunteering. Then this form appears. <break time="0.7s" /> Choose the Training Type, and set the Training Date. <break time="0.5s" /> Enter the Duration, in days. <break time="0.6s" /> Next, Valid Years — how long the certification lasts. For many courses this fills in automatically when you choose the type; leave it empty if the training never expires. <break time="0.6s" /> Add a Reference if you have one. <break time="0.6s" /> Then press Create Training to store the record.
