Before an I-D card can be printed, four things need to be in place.
<break time="0.6s" />
A photo, a signature, the membership fee paid, and a national I-D number.
<break time="0.6s" />
When you search for a person here, their card shows a red border if anything is missing.
<break time="0.6s" />
A blue border means everything is in place, and the card is ready.
<break time="0.6s" />
Prepare everything first, before requesting head office to print.
