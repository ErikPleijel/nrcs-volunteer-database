In this lesson, you will learn how to register records in the database. <break time="0.6s" /> There are four kinds of record you can add: Volunteering, Training, Payments, and Donations. <break time="0.7s" /> If you are a Division Database Assistant, what you can add depends on which kind you are. <break time="0.5s" /> An Operations assistant works with the activity records — Volunteering and Training. <break time="0.6s" /> A Finance assistant can record all four — Volunteering and Training, and also the financial records: Payments and Donations. <break time="0.6s" /> This distinction applies only at division level. Branch and national staff can register all four. <break time="0.7s" /> Let's look at each one in turn, starting with Volunteering.
