To add a volunteering record, start from the main menu. <break time="0.5s" /> Open Volunteering Log. <break time="0.6s" /> This brings you to the list of volunteering records. To add a new one, press the green Add Volunteering button. <break time="0.7s" /> First, you choose the person. In the search box, type their name, or their DB-number. <break time="0.6s" /> As you type, the database searches and shows the people who match. When you see the right one, click Select beside their name. <break time="0.6s" /> Now you are ready to enter the details.
