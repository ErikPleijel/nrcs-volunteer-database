Let's step back to the list for a moment — there is one more thing worth learning about filtering. <break time="0.7s" /> Filtering is how you narrow the list down to exactly the people you want. <break time="0.6s" /> You can combine as many filters as you like. In this example, we have set Gender to Male, and the age range from eighteen to thirty-five. <break time="0.6s" /> When you press Filter, the database shows how many people match — here, six hundred eighteen found. <break time="0.7s" /> Just below the count is the filter description. It restates your filters in plain words: Lifecycle — Active, Dormant; Male; Age eighteen to thirty-five. Always glance at this line to be sure you have set what you intended. <break time="0.7s" /> One thing to notice: even when you choose no lifecycle filter, the list shows only operational people — those who are active or dormant. Pending and archived people are left out, unless you ask for them.
