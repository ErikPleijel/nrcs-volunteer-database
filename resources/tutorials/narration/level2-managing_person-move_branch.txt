Sometimes a person needs to move to a different branch or division.
<break time="0.6s" />
Find the person using Search, then click Edit.
<break time="0.6s" />
Select the new Branch, or Division, in the dropdowns.
<break time="0.6s" />
Scroll down, and click Update Person.
<break time="0.6s" />
If the person holds a database admin role, that role must be removed first, before they can be moved.
<break time="0.6s" />
You may need to contact your Branch or Head Office to do this.
<break time="0.6s" />
Also note — Branch-level admins can only move people within their own branch.
