Let's start with assigning a person to a Red Cross Unit.
<break time="0.6s" />
Find the person using Search, then click Edit.
<break time="0.6s" />
Select their Red Cross Unit from the dropdown.
<break time="0.6s" />
If you can't find the right unit, you may need to change the Division first.
<break time="0.6s" />
Scroll down, and click Update Person.
<break time="0.6s" />
Once assigned, the person automatically moves out of Pending Engagement, and becomes Active.
<break time="0.6s" />
This is the defining step. Assigning someone to a Red Cross Unit is what makes them a volunteer in the system.
