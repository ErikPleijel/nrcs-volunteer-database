Now, Campaign Settings — these control how campaigns are sent, system-wide.
<break time="0.6s" />
Pay close attention to "Allowed link domains".
<break time="0.6s" />
This is a comma-separated list of domains allowed in campaign content links.
<break time="0.6s" />
Keep this list deliberate — it's a safeguard on what links can go out to members and volunteers.
<break time="0.6s" />
You'll also find a Daily email sending cap, and a Daily S-M-S sending cap.
<break time="0.6s" />
These set the maximum number of messages sent per day, before sending pauses automatically.
