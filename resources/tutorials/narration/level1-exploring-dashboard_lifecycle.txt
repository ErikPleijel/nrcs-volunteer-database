The Lifecycle Overview shows three boxes — Pending engagement, Active, and Dormant.
<break time="0.6s" />
Pending engagement means someone has registered but hasn't been guided to the next step yet.
<break time="0.6s" />
Active means they're engaged and contributing.
<break time="0.6s" />
Dormant means they were active before, but have gone quiet.
<break time="0.6s" />
Here's the challenge — keep the Active box as full as possible.
<break time="0.6s" />
Move people out of Pending and out of Dormant, and into Active.
<break time="0.6s" />
Click "Take action" on any box for step-by-step tips on exactly what to do.
