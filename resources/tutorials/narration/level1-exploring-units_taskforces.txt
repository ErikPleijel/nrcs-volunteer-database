Two views cover the groups people belong to — Red Cross Units, and Task Forces.
<break time="0.6s" />
In both views, you can filter by branch to narrow things down, or search by name to find a specific group.
<break time="0.6s" />
Click View on any row to see who belongs to it, along with their team leader and assistant team leader.
<break time="0.6s" />
Now, remember the difference between the two.
<break time="0.6s" />
A Red Cross Unit is the permanent home for volunteers — it's where they belong, and where their activity is recorded.
<break time="0.6s" />
A task force is different. It's temporary, created for a specific purpose, and open to anyone.
<break time="0.6s" />
Once its work is done, a task force is dissolved. A unit stays.
