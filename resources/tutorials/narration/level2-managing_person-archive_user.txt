Sometimes a person has permanently left the organisation.
<break time="0.6s" />
Find the person using Search, then click Edit.
<break time="0.6s" />
Scroll down, and tick "Archive this user".
<break time="0.6s" />
Click Update Person.
<break time="0.6s" />
Use this for someone who has left for good — not just gone quiet for a while.
<break time="0.6s" />
Archived users are hidden from the list. Use the "Show archived" button to find them again.
<break time="0.6s" />
Note — you cannot archive your own account.
