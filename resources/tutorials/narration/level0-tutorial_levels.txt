The training is divided into three levels, matching the access levels in the database. <break time="0.7s" /> Level 1 — Getting Started. Available to all staff with dashboard access. It covers the basics of using the database. <break time="0.6s" /> Level 2 — Branch Administration. For branch and national staff. It covers branch-level tasks, reports, and user management. <break time="0.6s" /> Level 3 — National Administration. For national staff only. It covers printing of ID cards, settings, and system-wide operations. <break time="0.7s" /> Your progress through each lesson is saved automatically when you reach the last slide. You can leave and return at any time.
