In this database, every person is either a member or a volunteer. <break time="0.5s" /> The difference is simple. A member supports the Red Cross by paying a membership fee. A member is not attached to a volunteer unit. <break time="0.6s" /> A volunteer serves in the field, through a Red Cross unit. Volunteers do the hands-on work — first aid, community activities, responding when help is needed. <break time="0.7s" /> So, the question to ask is: does this person belong to a Red Cross unit? If yes, they are a volunteer. If no, and they have paid their fee, they are a member.
