Step four is Message.
<break time="0.6s" />
For email, fill in the From name, Reply-to email, Subject, and the Email body.
<break time="0.6s" />
Use "Write" for simple messages, or switch to "Code" for full H-T-M-L newsletters.
<break time="0.6s" />
You can insert placeholders — like a person's first name — from the dropdown.
<break time="0.6s" />
For S-M-S, write your message and keep an eye on the character count, shown as characters and number of S-M-S parts.
<break time="0.6s" />
Both email and S-M-S automatically get a footer appended.
<break time="0.6s" />
Email gets a profile and login link, plus unsubscribe information. S-M-S gets an opt-out link.
