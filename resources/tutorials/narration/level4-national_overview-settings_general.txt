Let's look at National Database Settings.
<break time="0.6s" />
Under Membership, you can set how many months of inactivity pass before a volunteer becomes Dormant.
<break time="0.6s" />
Members with a paid fee work differently — they become Dormant when their payment expires, not from this setting.
<break time="0.6s" />
Under Site, you can set the Site Motto, shown in the footer and on public pages.
<break time="0.6s" />
Under Social, you can set the Social Share Snippet — the H-T-M-L used for social sharing meta tags.
