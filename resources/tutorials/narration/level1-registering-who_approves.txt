Not every role that can add a record can also approve one.
<break time="0.6s" />
A Branch Secretary or Branch D-B Administrator can approve records for their own branch.
<break time="0.6s" />
The National D-B Administrator can approve any record, from any branch.
<break time="0.6s" />
Division D-B Assistants, Branch D-B Assistants, and National D-B Assistants can only submit records — not approve them.
<break time="0.6s" />
Observers can't add or approve anything at all.
<break time="0.6s" />
And the four-eyes rule still applies within a branch.
<break time="0.6s" />
If a Branch Secretary submits a record themselves, only the Branch D-B Administrator or National D-B Administrator can approve it.
