
At the top left of the page, you will see a Search box. <break time="0.5s" /> You can use it to find a person by their name, their email address, or their DB-number. <break time="0.7s" /> So what is a DB-number? It is a unique code the database gives to every person, so that no two people are ever confused with one another. <break time="0.6s" /> It is built from four parts. For example: DB, seven four four two, LAG, Ikeja. <break time="0.6s" /> The number is the person's own identity number. LAG is the branch code — here, Lagos. And Ikeja is the division. <break time="0.7s" /> When you have typed what you are looking for, press the Filter button to see the results.
