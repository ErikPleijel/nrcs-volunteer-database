Let's look at the National D-B Administrator — the highest working role in the database itself.
<break time="0.6s" />
A National D-B Administrator authorizes and oversees the whole system.
<break time="0.6s" />
This includes appointing Branch Secretaries, and Branch D-B Administrators.
<break time="0.6s" />
National D-B Administrators themselves are appointed, or removed, through the Super Administrator account.
