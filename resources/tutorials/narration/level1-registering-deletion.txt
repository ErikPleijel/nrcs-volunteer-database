Sometimes a record is entered by mistake and needs to be removed. <break time="0.6s" /> To delete a record, first open it. Click View to go to the record's own page, and scroll to the very bottom. There you will find the Delete button. <break time="0.7s" /> Two things are worth knowing. <break time="0.5s" /> First, deleting here is what we call a soft delete. The record is hidden from the lists, but it is not truly erased — it stays safely in the database, and can be restored if the deletion was a mistake. <break time="0.7s" /> Second, every deletion is logged. The system records who deleted the record, and when — so there is always a clear trail. <break time="0.7s" /> Delete with care — but know that nothing is ever lost for good.
