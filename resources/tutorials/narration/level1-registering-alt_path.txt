There is another way to reach the entry forms, which is sometimes easier. <break time="0.6s" /> Start from a person's record — open Persons, search for them, and click View. <break time="0.6s" /> Scroll down to the lists. In each section — Membership, Volunteering, Training, and Donations — you will see an add button in its header. <break time="0.7s" /> Press the one you need, and you go straight to that entry form, with the person already selected. <break time="0.7s" /> So when you are already looking at someone's record, you can add a payment, a training, a donation, or a volunteering activity — without searching for them again.
