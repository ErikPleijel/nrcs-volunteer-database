The Archive Tool lets you deactivate inactive persons in bulk, instead of one by one.
<break time="0.6s" />
It keeps the database clean, and it saves cost — sending campaign messages to people who won't respond adds up.
<break time="0.6s" />
Here's how it works.
<break time="0.6s" />
Set an inactivity threshold — for example, four years.
<break time="0.6s" />
You'll see a list of persons, their last activity, and whether they've already been targeted by a campaign.
<break time="0.6s" />
Use this to make a quick judgment, person by person, on whether they should be archived.
<break time="0.6s" />
And remember — archived users can always be reactivated later. Archiving is not permanent.
