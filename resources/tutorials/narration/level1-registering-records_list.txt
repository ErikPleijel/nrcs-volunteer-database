Each kind of record has its own list — for volunteering, training, payments, and donations. Here we are looking at Training Records. <break time="0.6s" /> Every row is one record. To find the one you want, open the Filter and Sort panel. It is hidden by default, so click it to reveal the options. <break time="0.7s" /> From there you can search by name, reference, or training type, and filter by branch, division, or status — for example, only the trainings that are about to expire. <break time="0.7s" /> Two filters are especially handy. You can show only the records that you yourself entered. And you can include deleted records — to see what has been removed, or view everything together. <break time="0.6s" /> When you find the record you want, press View to open it in full.
