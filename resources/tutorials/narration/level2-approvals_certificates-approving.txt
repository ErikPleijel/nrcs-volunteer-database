Payments, volunteering logs, trainings, and donations all need approval before they count.
<break time="0.6s" />
Check the bottom of the Dashboard to see how many approvals are pending.
<break time="0.6s" />
Go to the relevant records list — for example, Payment Records — and open the Approvals tab.
<break time="0.6s" />
From there, click Approve, or Reject.
<break time="0.6s" />
If you reject a record, a popup asks you to give a reason.
<break time="0.6s" />
The submitter will be notified with the reason you give.
