Finally, Campaign Purposes.
<break time="0.6s" />
Each purpose — like Membership Pre-Expiry Notice, Training Invitation, or Welcome and Onboarding — has a default email subject, email body, and S-M-S body.
<break time="0.6s" />
These are pre-filled automatically when a branch selects that purpose in the campaign wizard, saving them from writing a message from scratch.
<break time="0.6s" />
Placeholders, like "user dot first name", are substituted automatically at send time — with the person's real name, branch, membership status, and more.
