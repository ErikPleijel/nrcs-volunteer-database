A few notes on printing.
<break time="0.6s" />
For pre-printed paper, there's a layout editor in the top right corner.
<break time="0.6s" />
It lets you adjust exactly where the text lands on the page — and your setting is remembered for next time.
<break time="0.6s" />
Important — once you've printed one or more certificates successfully, don't leave the page yet.
<break time="0.6s" />
Click "Mark as Printed". This is how the database keeps track of every printout.
<break time="0.6s" />
Click "View Print History" to see past printouts — and to correct any that were marked printed by mistake.
<break time="0.6s" />
One more thing — training certificates come in two kinds.
<break time="0.6s" />
Attendance confirms someone was present.
<break time="0.6s" />
Competence is a certification — it confirms they've been assessed, and met the standard.
<break time="0.6s" />
And finally — some certificates can only be printed at H-Q level.
