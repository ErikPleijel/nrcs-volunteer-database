Here's a common scenario.
<break time="0.6s" />
A person calls you — they tried to log in, but got a message that their account is archived.
<break time="0.6s" />
Archived users are hidden from the list, so use the "Show archived" button to find them.
<break time="0.6s" />
Find the person using Search, then click Edit.
<break time="0.6s" />
Scroll down, and untick "Archive this user".
<break time="0.6s" />
Click Update Person.
<break time="0.6s" />
The person returns to Active or Dormant status.
