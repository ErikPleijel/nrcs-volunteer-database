Let's look at managing Red Cross Units.
<break time="0.6s" />
Open Red Cross Units from the main menu.
<break time="0.6s" />
Press "Add New Unit" to create one, or Edit to update an existing one.
<break time="0.6s" />
Under Leadership Assignment, set the Team Leader and Assistant Team Leader.
<break time="0.6s" />
A unit no longer in use can be archived from the same page, and reactivated later if needed.
