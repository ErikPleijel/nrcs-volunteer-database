You can also set up the filter yourself, for more control.
<break time="0.6s" />
Go to Persons, and set a filter. For example, a specific Division, Volunteers only, and the age group Youth.
<break time="0.6s" />
Click Filter, and check how many records were found.
<break time="0.6s" />
If the group isn't too large, click "Make campaign from filter".
<break time="0.6s" />
If the audience is too big, it's better to narrow the filter first, rather than send to everyone at once.
